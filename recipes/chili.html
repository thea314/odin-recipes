Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 4 cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans red kidney beans (or try making yours from dry beans!)
  • 1 onion
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 3 dried chili peppers
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • worcestershire sauce
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Dice onion, crush garlic, and fry with ground beef
  2. add tomatoes and beans to pot
  3. Dice Bell pepper and add to pot
  4. Once meat is browned, drain fat and add to pot
  5. Add chili peppers
  6. Add chili powder, cumin, worcestershire sauce, and salt to taste
  7. Let stew several hours
