Stirfry

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium Onion
  • 1 cup white mushrooms
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 1 pound of sliced chicken breast
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • sambal
  • sesame oil
  • cocount oil

Steps:

  1. Heat coconut oil in wok
  2. Slice onions and add to hot oil
  3. Slice carrots and celery and add to wok
  4. add chicken
  5. Slice mushrooms and add to wok
  6. pulverize ginger and add
  7. stir over high heat
  8. add sesame oil, sambal, and soy sauce to taste
